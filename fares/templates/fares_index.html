Fares

An overview of fares data. This page isn’t very useful – eventually, fares data will be integrated into other parts of the website.

Known issues:

  • Some data confuses pounds and pence – e.g. a £3 fare is given as £0.03
  • Fare stages may have slightly different names in different bits of data. bustimes.org isn’t clever enough to work out they’re the same yet
  • bustimes.org only supports point to point fares so far, not day/week/month/zone tickets
